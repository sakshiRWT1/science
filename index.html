Science Park

Science Park is nothing but a cluster of open air Science gadgets all meant for play way learning of Science. Science Park is an innovative concept of teaching Science in an informal way. The basic concept of Science park lies on the tendency of the children to play more than to read.

World‑renowned educationists strive to develop tools for active learning. A‑One Science Park’s gadgets are designed so that children mingle with science naturally. From mechanics to meteorology, every exhibit sparks curiosity, enhances critical thinking, and builds a strong foundation. The park’s design aligns with NCERT / CBSE curiosity‑based learning, turning abstract concepts into tangible experiences.
