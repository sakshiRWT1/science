STEP 01 — PLAY-WAY PHILOSOPHY

What is a Science Park?

A-One Science Park is a cluster of open‑air science gadgets designed for play‑way learning of Science. It’s an innovative concept that turns education into an informal, engaging experience. Children naturally love to play — this park turns that instinct into scientific discovery. Educationists, scientists, and psychiatrists endorse play‑way methods, and our science park covers mechanics, sound, light, heat, chemistry, biology, meteorology & applied sciences.

✔ Inculcates scientific temper among children and adults.
✔ Ideal for school campuses & public parks.
✔ 300+ hands‑on exhibits.

STEP 03 — FASCINATING GADGET

 MASS & INERTIA

Product Description: The equilibrium of any object is governed by the position of its centre of gravity. This fascinating model explains it with fun. Watch the cone seemingly roll upward — a classic demonstration of gravity and balance.

Recommended Class: VII, VIII, IX
Dimensions (l×b×h): 10" × 3" × 3"

★ Visitors love to see the illusion of the cone moving against gravity — perfect for understanding centre of mass.

STEP 04 — RELATION BETWEEN MASS & INERTIA

FRICTION & SPEED

Product Description: With three rotating discs of iron, aluminum, and acrylic, this exhibit beautifully illustrates the relationship between mass and inertia. Spin them to feel how heavier objects resist change in motion.

Recommended Class: VII, VIII, IX, X
Dimensions (l×b×h): 4" × 2" × 5.5"

✔ Safe play area — durable materials for outdoor use.
✔ Direct observation of Newton’s first law in action.
